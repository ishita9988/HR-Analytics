## Introduction

This ia a Power BI project on HR Analytics aimed at analyzing and deriving insights on the factors that impact attrition. The primary objective of the project is to assist the company in determining the necessary actions to take to retain more employees.

## Dataset

The dataset contains important informations like - Age of employee, Department, Job role, Educational Background, Gender, Monthly income, Relationship Satisfaction, Performance rating, Years at company, Work life balance, etc.

## Tools

* **Power Query Editor**
* **Power BI**

## Modelling

No modelling required

## Project Workflow

* The data is cleaned and transformed using Power Query. 
* Dashboard is created with visualizations like pie chart, area chart and bar charts.
* Insights

## Data Transformation

Various steps has been performed like removing duplicates, replacing values, changing datatype and removing unnecessary columns.

## Insights

* Employees with educational background in live sciences has the highest attrition rate of 38% followed by medical background with 27%
* Employees in the age group of 26-35 has the highest attrition count.
* Employees with monthly salary less than 5k has highest attrition count.
* Most of the employees leave the company within 2 years of joining.
* The attrition count of laboratory technicial is highest followed by sales executive
